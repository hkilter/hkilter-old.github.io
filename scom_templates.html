SCOM Templates

Template - Member Boxes

There two templates that show different perspectives about members. First template, Member Box - Basic, shows two points of interest for a certain member. There are (1) Received elements (incoming shipment, returned product, order and money) and (2) Sent elements (outgoing shipment, returned product, order and money) parts for a member to interact its respective neighbours (supplier, transporter, distributor etc.) Member Box - Advanced is the second template and it shows inner dynamics (operations) of the member itself.

Template - Flow Types

There are three types of flow; (1) Information, (2) Materials and (3) Money.
